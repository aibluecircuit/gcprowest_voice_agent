# Squarespace Deployment Guide - GC Pro West AI Agent

To add the AI Voice Agent to your Squarespace website, follow these steps:

### 1. Open Squarespace Code Injection
1. Log in to your Squarespace account and open your website editor.
2. Go to **Settings** > **Advanced** > **Code Injection**.

### 2. Paste the Code
1. Scroll down to the **Footer** section.
2. Open the file `GCProWest_Agent_Squarespace.html` (located in your project folder).
3. Copy **everything** inside that file.
4. Paste it into the **Footer** box in Squarespace.
5. Click **Save** at the top left.

### 3. Verify
1. Visit your live website.
2. You should see the black circular button with a microphone icon (and the "Andy" avatar) in the bottom right corner.
3. Click it to start talking to your AI assistant!

> [!NOTE]
> The backend is hosted on Render which may "sleep" if inactive. The first time you click the button, it might take 30-60 seconds for the backend to wake up and connect.
